🍽️About Alburuj Restaurant

🍴Alburuj was inaugurated on 12 July 2020.

📖The name is taken from the Surah Al-Buruj of the Quran.

👤The owner of the restaurant is Mr. Irfan Yousuf from Anantnag.

🌏Alburuj provides the best varieties in Indian, Chinese, Continental, and famous Kashmiri cuisine Wazwan.

👨‍🍳We have hired the best chefs across the valley, and all meals are prepared fresh from locally sourced mutton and chicken

🚫No frozen meat or chicken is ever used at Alburuj — that’s why our name stands tall in the town!


🍽️ Our Philosophy At Alburuj, we believe dining is not just about food—it’s about creating memorable experiences. Every dish is crafted with love, passion, and attention to detail, ensuring every meal delights your taste buds.
